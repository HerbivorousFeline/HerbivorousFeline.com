The MHA OC Discord / subreddit RP is designed to allow users to creatively design their OC and then participate within the world of Heroes with other players. ------------------------------------------------------ The Setting What does it take to be a hero? Ratings On the OC template there is a section at the end for “Ratings”. Character ratings should be used more as a basic guideline during Role Playing. However, if something is more narratively interesting and the other players agree, the Ratings can be worked around to fulfill a desired result. Each player can assign up to 15 points in total to their Ratings at first. The Ratings are as follows: Power – Raw Quirk power and/or Physical strength Speed – Physical speed and/or reaction speed Technique – Versatility of quirk use, quirk control and combat knowledge Intelligence - perception, knowledge, wisdom, creativity Cooperativeness - charisma, insight, sociability ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ (edited)
Dice Pool Ratings can be utilized through a “Dice Pool”. A player’s Dice Pool starts at D4 (4 sided dice). When a rolling, such as during a competition of strength against an opponent, attempting to determine an opponent’s intentions, etc, the user will utilize a number of dice equal to their Rating. So, if they are attempting to arm wrestle an opponent, both players roll the number of dice equal to their Power Rating. Whosever number is higher wins and can determine the narrative outcome. Preferably the dice will be utilize sparingly and both players can agree on a narrative direction.This is optional and should be utilized when both players agree to roll. When you wish to roll, please use the following (! 1d4). Example: I am punching an opponent. My Power is 4. I roll 4d4. My results is 12. My opponent’s Power is 3 and they attempt to block. They roll 3d4 and their result is 9. My roll is higher, so my attack works. (edited)
By completing events or utilizing designated “Character Improvement” threads, which will occur every so often, players can increase their Rating scores and increase the type of dice utilize for rolls, gaining dice with more sides. These rewards are at the discretion of the mods and will be rewarded for creativity, well written character development, or other special events. 5 is the maximum and 1 is the minimum number a player can have in any one Rating. There will be exceptions or special circumstances, but they will be rare. Only those without quirks can have a 0 in a Rating.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- FAQ 1) Do I have to use the template from the Reddit for my OC RP submission? - Yes. We prefer you fill out our template to the best of your abilities. 2) Can I use a character for RP in a higher grade than year one? - Nope. All newly accepted RP submissions will start as year 1, with a rating total of 15, and with their Dice Pool at d4. There will be chances to increase the size of dice and, on rare occasions, rating numbers. 3) How will I know when events are going to occur? - We will let you know a week or so in advanced. In addition, there will be a calendar of events you can check in the future. (edited)

4) Are there some Quirks which will be scrutinized harder than others? - Yes. Quirks such as the following have the potential to be game breaking and as such will be more heavily scrutinized and in some cases, rejected: +Wide array of varied effects, elemental components, or read like multiple quirks +Effects that can manipulate another player (such as mind control, body control, or quirk control) +Time travel or manipulation +Zero point energy manipulation +Creation of matter from nothing.
