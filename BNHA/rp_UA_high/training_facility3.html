February 25th The chill in the air bites at the students as they make their way to their assigned training ground. With the written exam out of the way, the only thing left in their way is the practical exam. The Principal of UA, Ise Hanekage, -an owl wearing a monocle and an Oxford cap who presented their written exam and then provided instruction on where to go afterwards- had indicated this year was to be a combat based exam. As the prospective students gathered in front of a massive gate, murmurs could be heard wondering what kind of combat exam could await them beyond the enormous walls. On top of one of the walls a massive monitor could be seen. The screen suddenly flipped on. A man wearing a reddish brown tunic, red cape with a tall collar, red wide brimmed hat, and brown Domino mask was revealed. It was the pro hero Red Mage, a known teacher at UA. He smiled as he looked down at the group.
"Welcome! You lucky few have made it to the next leg of this illustrious opportunity!" He said loudly, with sweeping, grandiose arms. He bowed low like an English gentleman, before popping up and tipping his hat. "As you all know, this is the most important exam of your student lives. It will determine your very future! Everything you've strove for, all your heroic hopes and endeavours are all determined by this moment!" He announced, a thespian giving the performance of a lifetime. He brought a hand to his chin and cupped it between his fingers. "Actually, this reminds me of a wonderful story I…" Suddenly, from off screen, a stern, young woman's voice could be heard interrupting, "Get to the point!" Jumping, he looked embarrassed for a second and scratched the back of his head, his voice going up a pitch in nervousness, "Ah, yes, of course." He chuckled, then cleared his throat and turned back to the camera.
"Unlike previous years, there will be no visible point system this year. Our panel of staff will evaluate you in real-time based on the amount of enemies you take down, in addition to other criteria. You may be wondering, what is the criteria? Well, you'll find out at the end!" He turned slightly, allowing for a visual of four different robots to pop up beside him. "Your enemies will be the following training robot models: TRS Model- Small Robot: Multiple Visible Off Buttons and Easily Destroyed or DeDeactivat TRM Model - Medium Robot: Single Visible Off Button and Moderately Difficult to Destroy or Deactivate. TRL Model - Large Robot: Single Hidden Off Button and Very Difficult to Destroy or Deactivate TRC Model- Colossal Robot: No Off Button and Impossible for a Student to Destroy or Deactivate. They will attack in 4 waves. It will be beneficial to make it through all four waves!" Red Mage declared. A timer then appeared. "You will have 1 hour. The doors will open in 5 minutes. Prepare yourselves, and good luck!" He declared with another bow and a dashing smile, before the screen turned off. (edited)
